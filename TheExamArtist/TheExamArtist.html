What is the Exam Artist?

The Exam Artist is a VR Stealth game where you are a student who tried to finish an exam you know nothing of with various ways and without getting caught by teacher. Created in Unity VR

How to play

Use Oculus Controller to point and trigger to select answer/pick up projectile. Various buttons to use skills. (SteamVR required). You can also use mouse left click and drag around to click. Spacebar to show skills.

Peeking

You have at least one neighbor to peek at.
You might not clearly see the question, so
it is time to present your wisdom to
find out which question he/she is writing.

‘Magic’ Cheat Sheet

Your cheat sheet is in your drawer.
It can show where the answers are located inside the classroom.
Follow instruction to see the answers if you can.

Washroom Time

I am in a hurry! I need to go to the washroom please, teacher!
My buddy put a note in the washroom. He might have found some answers for the test!

Bribe?

There are three or less students that agreed to help me.
It’s time to let them help me distract the teacher!

Meditation

I am sure I’ve seen or studied this question before…
Let me think hard to find out the answer...

Time Freeze

A new device I got from a strange-looking doctor…
I can freeeezzeee time for 10 seconds, how awesome!
